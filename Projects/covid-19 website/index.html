What is novel coronavirus?

SARS-CoV-2 is a coronavirus that causes coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19). Coronaviruses are a family of viruses that target and affect mammals’ respiratory systems. There are four main ranks of coronaviruses: alpha, beta, delta, and gamma. how to prevent?

wear a mask

Wearing a high-quality mask and physical distancing, helps protect you and others by reducing the chance of spreading COVID-19.

wash your hand

Washing hands can keep you healthy and prevent the spread of respiratory and diarrheal infections.

consult doctor

COVID-19 is a respiratory disease whose symptoms range from mild to moderate, it can lead to serious repercussions, so must consult to doctor.

avoid touching

To reduce the spread of COVID-19, it is recommend not to touch face, as it can transmit through mucous membranes in the mouth, nose and eyes.

avoid contact

Avoiding contact with people who have COVID-19, whether or not they feel sick, can reduce your risk of catching the virus from them.

clean everyday

Launder items with soap or detergent, using the warmest appropriate water setting and dry items completely for prevention.
